Expressing yourself through music can ease tension and lead you down new paths of inspiration. Greg Monk, Ashton, and I made it our mission to bring together our individual tastes of music, perspectives on what it was to be from the country, and desire to perform memorable music in fun environments. Somewhere along the line I took it upon myself to download Photoshop and Illustrator and start making the some of the dumbest looking folk posters around, but in the process, I ended up making some decent images. The most important product of this venture is that I recognized what collaborating with good people can do for you. For that I will always be thankful to the Country Gentlemen.
